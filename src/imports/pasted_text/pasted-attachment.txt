Create a premium, modern, international restaurant website design for a fictional brand called “LUMÉ Dining”.

The website should feel elegant, global, warm, luxurious, and conversion-focused. It should not look country-specific. Avoid Indian, Italian, Chinese, or any single-cuisine identity. Make it suitable for an international restaurant in cities like London, Dubai, Singapore, New York, Toronto, or Sydney.

Brand concept:
LUMÉ Dining is a modern global restaurant offering chef-crafted dishes, elegant interiors, seasonal ingredients, cocktails, brunch, private dining, reservations, and online ordering.

Brand personality:
Premium, warm, stylish, modern, calm, sophisticated, global, welcoming.

Target audience:
Couples, tourists, families, business diners, event planners, food lovers, hotel guests, and people looking for elegant casual dining.

Primary business goals:
1. Get more table reservations.
2. Get more online orders.
3. Get more calls and directions.
4. Promote private dining and events.
5. Build trust through reviews, gallery, and strong branding.

Design style:
Use a dark premium theme with warm luxury accents.
Use large food photography, elegant spacing, clean typography, rounded cards, subtle shadows, smooth sections, and a high-end restaurant atmosphere.

Color palette:
- Deep charcoal: #111111
- Warm ivory: #F8F4EC
- Champagne gold: #C8A96A
- Clay terracotta: #B66A50
- Soft gray: #8E8E8E
- White: #FFFFFF

Typography:
Use an elegant serif font for headings, similar to Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or Lora.
Use a clean sans-serif font for body text, similar to Inter, Manrope, or Poppins.
